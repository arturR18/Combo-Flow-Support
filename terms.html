1. Description of Service

Combo Flow is a mobile application designed to assist with boxing and Muay Thai training. The App provides:

  • 94 pre-programmed training combinations with difficulty ratings (1-10 scale)
  • Advanced difficulty tracking system based on combo complexity and movement types
  • Customizable timer systems for rounds and rest periods
  • Audio and haptic feedback for training cues
  • The ability to create and save custom combinations and moves
  • Training session tracking and history
  • Hands-free control through proximity sensors
  • Advanced analytics dashboard (Pro feature)
  • Weekly goal tracking and training streaks
  • 12-month training heatmap visualization (Pro feature)
  • Technique analysis and training insights (Pro feature)

The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection for core functionality.
