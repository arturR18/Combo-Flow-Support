Combo Flow

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Simple Summary: Combo Flow collects absolutely NO personal data. The app works 100% offline. All your training data, analytics, and custom combos stay on your device. We don't track you, we don't analyze you, we don't sell your data - because we don't have any of it.

Age Requirement: Combo Flow is designed for users aged 9 and older. The app contains combat sports training content that may not be suitable for younger children.

1. Information We Collect

None.

Combo Flow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. This includes but is not limited to:

  • No personal identification information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No training statistics sent to servers
  • No social media integration
  • No cloud synchronization

2. How The App Works

Combo Flow operates entirely offline on your device:

  • Local Storage Only: All your data is stored locally on your device in the app's sandboxed storage area, including:
    • 94 built-in combos with difficulty ratings
    • Custom combos and moves (unlimited for Pro users)
    • Training session history and analytics
    • Difficulty calculations and progress tracking
    • Weekly goals and training streaks
    • Technique analysis and training insights
  • No Internet Required: The app functions completely without an internet connection.
  • No Account Creation: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.
  • No External Communication: The app never communicates with external servers or services.
  • Session Tracking: Training sessions are counted locally after 60 seconds or when Round 1 starts. Free users have 2 sessions per week (resets Monday 00:00 local time).

3. Device Permissions

Combo Flow may request the following device permissions to enhance your training experience:

Haptic Feedback (Optional)

Used to provide tactile feedback during training sessions. This permission only controls your device's vibration motor and does not collect any data.

Audio (Core Feature)

Used to play beep sounds for round starts, combo changes, and rest periods. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

Proximity Sensor (Optional)

Used for hands-free control during training. Allows you to pause, skip, or repeat combos by hovering your hand near the device. No proximity data is stored or transmitted.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is ensured through:

  • iOS Sandboxing: All app data is stored in iOS's secure sandboxed environment.
  • File Protection: Data files are protected with iOS's complete file protection, making them inaccessible when your device is locked.
  • No Backup to iCloud: App data is explicitly excluded from iCloud backups to ensure your training data stays only on your device.
  • Local Only: Since no data leaves your device, there's no risk of data breaches from server compromises.

6. Third-Party Services

None.

Combo Flow does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms.

7. Children's Privacy

Combo Flow does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, because it doesn't collect any information at all. The app is safe for users of all ages from a privacy perspective.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app immediately removes all associated data
  • You can reset the app data at any time through the iOS Settings app
  • There is no data to request deletion from servers because we don't have any servers storing your data

9. International Users

Since Combo Flow doesn't collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfer concerns. Your data stays on your device regardless of your location.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Since the app doesn't collect data, we cannot notify you directly of changes, so we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Our commitment to zero data collection will never change. If that ever needs to change for any reason, it would be clearly communicated and would require your explicit consent.

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data:

  • There's no data for you to access, correct, or delete from our servers
  • There's no data to port to another service
  • There's no data processing to object to
  • You have complete control over your data because it never leaves your device
